Igri

Igri are builders, with a strictly organized and orthodox society protecting people from the harsh dangers of life.

Niji

Quick witted and feral Niji follow their own path and consider themselves free spirits.

Kyndi

Ancient Enigmatic Kyndi were custodians of life on Ril. Today's Kyndi are the few remaining descendants of these originals.
