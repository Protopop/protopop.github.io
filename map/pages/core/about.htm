What's Nimian Legends?

Nimian Legends is the fantasy setting for Nimian games and is created by Robert Kabwe. It's a labor of love, created in my spare time to bring the world of Nimian Legends to life. Since I was a kid playing Dungeons & Dragons I've loved creating worlds and maps.
