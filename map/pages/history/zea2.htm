The Assasina
The Assasina is made up largely of former slaves who naturally oppose the Empyres slavery laws and call for the death of Emperoro Mortelle himself. Their clan symbol is brazenly the severed head of the Emperoro, a reminder that the Empyre is rife with instabilities that threaten to tear it apart.
Public Relations
Over a decade ago the Emperoro was saved from an assasination attempt by a lone Igri adventurer. Ever grateful the Emperoro has made many overtures to the Igri community to increaingly integrate them into the culture of the Empyre.
Savar Seceeds
The region of Savar has left the Empyre, throwing the balance of power into dissarray. With the Open Coast and Savar exiting, the Empyre has lost much of its influence. Savar has not been hard hot by the Plague, but it remains to see how ling this will last with the protection of Emperoro Mortelles ruthless anti-plague activities.
