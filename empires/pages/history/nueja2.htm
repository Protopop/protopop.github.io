Door Puzzles
Some dungeons doors can be quite tricky to open. This is when you need a Rune Cracker - there are different kinds in the gear store in Hivre and other villages. They'll make the job of entering dungeons much simpler.
Wytches Welcome
In a land where the mayor is herself a Wytch (Wytchlin in Hivre) it's no surprise Wytches and their craft are so welcome in Koldemarsh. The area has also attracted Wytch Hunters, eager to prove their worth battling a wide variety of Wytches native to the area.
Death to Mortelle
Few places in the Open Coast are as hostile to the Emperoro as Koldemarsh. His ruthless persecution of Wytchcraft and the burning of nearby villages to prevent plague have left this corner of the worl breeding ground for anti-empyrialist factions like the Assasina.
